Hello!

There is this game that I simply adore called Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. It is what is classified as a MOBA game, or a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. These are games that usually consists of 5v5s, popular titles including League of Legends or DOTA 2. Like games of these titles, MLBB consists of 5 roles for the five players, The Jungler, The Roamer, The Mid-Laner, The Gold-Laner, and The EXP-Laner. These 5 roles try to coordinate and destroy the opponent's Nexus on the other side of the map. This page will go in-depth on the details of 3 heroes that I commonly use in the game.

Mobile Legend: Bang Bang is a game developed by a Chinese company called Moonton in 2016. Initially shot down multiple times due to copyright concerns from Riot, it finally deleted most of its plagiarized content and became a mainstream mobile game all over the world.
