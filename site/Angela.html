Angela: "Bunnylove"

Summary

Angela is one of the most popular healer in MLBB. You could build her to either poke the enemies or to support allies. Her most notable feature is her ult, which could attach herself to an ally, granting them both shield and heals. She could also cast skills on top of the ally, nullifying the necassary mana cost. She is infamous in MLBB for being the hero often played by the girl in couple duos.

Roles

The Support role consists of heroes that could buff and heal allies in teamfights. Great as Roamers.

The Mage role consists of heroes that could deal skill magic damage to enemies. Great as Mid-Laners.

Lore

Angela is one of the heroes developed in Lab 1718, along with Saber and Alpha. However, she is the first mechanical being created without the need for a human body and soul. Dr. Baker, her 'father', believes that this is the first step to fulfill the lab's dream of using technology to bring peace and prosperity to the nation. However, the mad scientist and his gang in the lab secretly wanted to make flesh weapons to conquer the world. He ordered Dr.Bakers old creation, Alpha, to hunt down Dr.Baker and Angela. Dr.Baker, before being captured, but Angela in a capsule on a plane, telling her to find his old friend Rooney and escape from the clutches of the dark-side of the lab.
