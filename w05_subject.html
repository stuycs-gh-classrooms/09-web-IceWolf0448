Short MLBB Rant

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang(MLBB) is a mobile game beloved by many Filopinos.
Unfortunately, neither I or this country I am in identfies of Filipino. This
gave me very little gaming pals to play with late into the night. Hence, I'll
now write a short blog on heroes I play and cry in a corner.

  • Zilong

    This is the hero I have the most matches on in Mobile Legends, namely
    109. He is quite famous for being the second hero you unlock in the
    game, meaning everybody has him. He is pretty balanced, being able
    to do a lot of basic attack damage. However, that is kind of the reason
    for the existance of the Marksman class, so people belittle him as a
    close-range Fighter class marksman.

  • Benedetta

    This is the hero I have the second most matches on, which is 51. She
    is approximatly 20x harder to play than Zilong, she is classified as
    one of the hardest heroes in the game. Therefore, my winrate with her
    is only 47.06%, in contrast with my Zilong's 65.14% winrate. For some
    reason, I always feel intense pressure whenever I use her.

  • Edith

    This is my third most used hero with a usage rate of 45 matches. I
    am actually quite decent with her, with my winrate standing at
    57.78%. Her functions are actually quite interesting, she is
    normally a tank residing inside a huge suit of armor. During her
    ult, she pops out and becomes an electricity-shooting marksman.
    She is therefore capable of both defence of offence, making her
    part of the meta ever since her release.

  • Arlott

    This is actually one of my newer heroes, he is a fighter that
    benefits from building him full tank items. He is able to
    spam his skills as long as the enemy is stunned, and there
    is a tank item that deals damage based of % MAX HP, not to
    mention his lifesteal is also based of % MAX HP. I only have
    10 ranked matches with him with a winrate of 50%, but I think
    I'll fully master him pretty soon.
